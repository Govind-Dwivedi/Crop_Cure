Early blight, caused by Alternaria solani, is a common fungal disease of tomatoes grown in fields, greenhouses, and high tunnels. In warm, rainy and wet weather, epidemics of this disease can cause severe defoliation, yield loss, and poor fruit quality

Prevent This Plant Disease by follow below steps :
